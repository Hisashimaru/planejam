# Story
The world now enters a age of travel. Increasing in the number of airlines and the sky is filled by the air planes. 
Flight plans are useless. Avoiding crazy planes and carry passengers to the airport!  
You do not have a pilot license? No problem!! You can get a gold license while working.

# Controls (Keyboard)
Turning ..... A,D or Left,Right
Landing ..... Hold Space
Boost ....... Hold Shift

# Controls (XBOX Controller)
Turning ..... Left Stick
Landing ..... Hold A
Boost ....... Hold X
